## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Click on a piece to select it (only your pieces can be selected)
3. Valid moves will be highlighted in yellow
4. Click on a highlighted square to move your piece
5. The game alternates between white and black players
6. Click "New Game" to reset the board

## Game Rules

The game follows standard chess rules:
- **Pawns**: Move forward one square (two squares from starting position), capture diagonally
- **Rooks**: Move horizontally or vertically any number of squares
- **Knights**: Move in an "L" shape (2 squares in one direction, 1 in perpendicular)
- **Bishops**: Move diagonally any number of squares
- **Queen**: Combines rook and bishop movement
- **King**: Move one square in any direction

The game detects check and checkmate conditions automatically.
